###
**Rofi** and **JG Launcher** is searchable - filter by typing - and can also be navigated using the arrow keys.

* **Root** menu <kbd>Alt</kbd><kbd>Space</kbd> ***or*** Rightclick desktop
* **JG Launcher** <kbd>Super</kbd> or <kbd>Alt</kbd><kbd>F1</kbd> ***or*** using the Manjaro icon
* **Rofi** (<kbd>Alt</kbd><kbd>F2</kbd>)

* To add/change wallpaper use 
 **Preferences** &rarr; **Wallpaper** item.
* Changing themes, fonts etc is located in
 **Root** menu &rarr; **Preferences**
* Help and documentation is located in
 **Root** menu &rarr; **Help and Info**
